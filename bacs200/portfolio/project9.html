Vision

What is the vision of the BBB? Our vision is to create a more trustworthy global marketplace through enhancing values. Our main ways of doing this Include: Setting standards for marketplace trust Encouraging and supporting best practices by engaging with and educating consumers and businesses Celebrating marketplace role models Calling out and addressing substandard marketplace behavior Creating a community of trustworthy businesses and charities

Strategies

How do we achieve this goal? We will achieve this goal through allowing ease of access to the website and services. It is simple to file a complaint and to reach resolve. Our business practice regulation agents will be on the lines and monitoring the complaints to have the most prompt replies.
